About Us

Welcome to HOPE KOKENO Community-Based Organization (C.B.O)! Our organization was established with a heartfelt commitment to uplifting the lives of the less fortunate in Gwassi North Ward, Suba Sub County, HomaBay County, Kenya.

Read More

Mission

To empower and uplift impoverished households in Gwassi North Ward, Suba Sub County, HomaBay County, Kenya. We are dedicated to providing support, resources, and opportunities to the most vulnerable members of our community, particularly those affected by HIV/AIDS and widowed individuals.

Vision

To create a thriving and self-sufficient community in Gwassi North Ward, where every household has access to opportunities for growth, prosperity, and well-being. We envision a society where vulnerable households are empowered with the knowledge, resources, and support they need to overcome challenges and build sustainable livelihoods.
